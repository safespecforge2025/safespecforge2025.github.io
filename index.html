AV yields to predestrians near crosswalks. 


rule-ODD “passing crosswalk”
trigger av.on_crosswalk = True
rule if (av.ahead(npc) or av.next(npc)) and av.dist(npc) < 4.5 
        and npc.type = “pedestrian” 
     then av.max_speed(1.74)
exit-trigger av.on_crosswalk = False

AV interacts with driving NPCs when turning left in intersections. 


rule-ODD “passing intersection, turning left”
trigger av.in_intersection = True and av.turn_left = True
rule if (av.ahead(npc) or (av.next(npc) and av.right(npc)) and 
        av.dist(npc) >3.8 and av.dist(npc) < 8.62 and 
        npc.type = “vehicle” 
     then av.relative_speed_diff(npc) > -9.3 and 
          av.relative_speed_diff(npc) < 10.01
exit-trigger av.in_intersection = False
